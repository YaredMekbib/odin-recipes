Scrambled Eggs

Description

A simple, homemade recipe for scrambled that will make your mouth water

Ingredients

  • Two eggs
  • Half an onion
  • Three tablespoons of avocado oil
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. First place the avocado oil in the pan on medium heat
  2. Then dice the half onion and put it in the pan to grill
  3. Then crack two eggs on place them in the pan to scramble. Use a utensil to mix and mush it around
  4. After eggs begin to solidify, use black pepper and salt religiously
