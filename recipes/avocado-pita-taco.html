Avocado Pita Taco

Description

A simple, homemade recipe for an avocado pita taco that will make your mouth water

Ingredients

  • A full pita bread
  • Half an onion
  • A full tomato
  • A full avocado
  • Salt
  • Garlic Salt
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. First place dice the half onion and tomato finely
  2. Then cut the avocado in half and put it in a bowl with the diced tomato and onion
  3. Then mix and mush the content of the bowl thoroughly
  4. After mixing and mashing thoroughly add salt, garlic salt, and black pepper into the bowl
  5. Mix all ingredients again
  6. Warm up pita bread in the microwave
  7. Place content in bowl into pita bread opening and make a taco
